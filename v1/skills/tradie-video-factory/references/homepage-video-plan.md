# Homepage Video Plan

Create six editable HyperFrames projects for the homepage.

## 1. home-hero-front-desk-system

- Target duration: 3 minutes
- Purpose: explain the main promise and mechanism
- CTA: Book a Free Front Desk Audit
- Visuals: missed call line, enquiry pulse, five-step flow, owner alert, clear pipeline

## 2. home-good-jobs-slipping

- Target duration: 60-75 seconds
- Purpose: agitate missed calls, slow replies, cold quotes, scattered opportunities
- CTA: Check Where You're Losing Jobs
- Visuals: messy cards collapsing into a single line

## 3. home-front-desk-reframe

- Target duration: 75-90 seconds
- Purpose: teach “you may not need more leads first”
- CTA: Find the leaks before spending more
- Visuals: lead volume gauge vs front-desk leak map

## 4. home-five-step-flow

- Target duration: 90-120 seconds
- Purpose: explain Capture, Respond, Qualify, Book, Follow Up
- CTA: See how your enquiry flow works
- Visuals: five animated nodes with glowing connectors

## 5. home-system-modules

- Target duration: 60-90 seconds
- Purpose: summarize modules
- CTA: View What's Included
- Visuals: module tiles for missed call, response, quote form, follow-up, pipeline, reviews

## 6. home-audit-and-packages

- Target duration: 45-60 seconds
- Purpose: move viewers to audit and package fit
- CTA: Book My Free Front Desk Audit
- Visuals: audit checklist, package cards, flagship highlight
