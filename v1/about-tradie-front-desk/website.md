# 1. Purpose Of This Document

This document is the full website blueprint for **The Tradie Front Desk System**.

It is designed to give a website builder, AI website generator, copywriter, or developer everything needed to create a clear, high-converting, multi-page website.

The website must communicate the offer in plain English for trade business owners.

It should not feel like:

- A generic AI agency website
- A traditional advertising agency website
- A software dashboard website
- A lead-selling website
- A technical automation website
- A vague marketing consultant website

It should feel like:

> A practical, trustworthy, done-for-you front-desk system for tradies who want more booked jobs, less chasing, and fewer missed opportunities.

The website’s job is to make the right tradie think:

> This is exactly what my business needs. I do not need another app or ad agency. I need this front-desk system.

The design should feel:

- Modern
- Clean
- Trustworthy
- Practical
- Trade-focused
- Professional
- Clear
- Mobile-first

- Strong headline typography

- Trade/worksite imagery used carefully
- Simple icons for the system steps
- Clear CTA buttons
- High contrast
- Lots of spacing

# Home Section 11: FAQ

## Section Purpose

Handle major objections before they ask.

## FAQ 1: Is this just a lead generation service?

No. We do not sell shared leads. The system helps your business capture, respond to, follow up with, and organise its own enquiries. If you need more enquiry volume, campaigns can be added as a growth layer.

## FAQ 2: Do I have to run ads myself?

No. You do not have to run ads. The core system focuses on enquiry capture, response, follow-up, and booking. If ads are included, they are managed as part of the service.

## FAQ 3: Is AI required?

No. AI phone support is optional. Many businesses start with missed-call text-back, enquiry replies, follow-up messages, and pipeline tracking.

## FAQ 4: Can the system book jobs automatically?

It can, but many trade businesses prefer a callback or quote request process first. We can set the system up so you stay in control and review enquiries before confirming jobs.

## FAQ 5: What if I already have a job management system?

That is fine. Many job systems manage work after it is accepted. The Tradie Front Desk System focuses on the enquiry stage before the job is booked.

## FAQ 6: Can you guarantee more jobs?

We do not guarantee exact job numbers. Results depend on your market, services, pricing, availability, reputation, and customer demand. We guarantee the agreed system will be built, tested, and launched properly.
