# Tradie Front Desk Rebuilt Business System

This folder is the rebuilt business, offer, sales, proof, and website blueprint for **Tradie Front Desk**.

The purpose is to move the business away from technical GoHighLevel, CRM, AI, and automation language and into a plain-English offer that trade business owners understand quickly.

## Core Position

**Tradie Front Desk installs and manages a front-desk system for tradies that captures enquiries, replies fast, follows up, and helps turn quote requests into booked jobs.**

Short version:

**More booked jobs. Less chasing. No tech headaches.**

Stronger website version:

**Stop losing good jobs from missed calls, slow replies, and forgotten follow-up.**

## Strategic Rule

The public-facing business should not sell:

- GoHighLevel
- CRM
- AI agents
- Automations
- Funnels
- Workflows
- Ads
- Leads

The public-facing business should sell:

- Faster responses
- Fewer missed calls
- Better quote follow-up
- More booked jobs
- Less admin
- A clearer job pipeline
- A business system the owner does not have to build themselves

## Default Language

Use this language:

- enquiries
- quote requests
- missed calls
- booked jobs
- site visits
- callbacks
- follow-up
- front desk
- on the tools
- less chasing
- customer database
- old leads
- reviews

Avoid leading with this language:

- AI agent
- CRM
- workflow
- automation stack
- API
- funnel
- SaaS
- omnichannel
- bot
- dashboard

These technical terms can appear in delivery documents and deeper FAQs, but they should not lead the website, sales pitch, or ads.
