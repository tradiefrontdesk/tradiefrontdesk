# Sales Message And Objection Handling

## Sales Narrative

Tradie Front Desk should not open sales conversations with AI, automations, CRM, or ads.

Open with the owner's reality:

> You are busy on the tools. Calls get missed. Quote requests sit in email. Messages come through Facebook, Instagram, Google, texts, and the website. Quotes get sent and then forgotten. The result is simple: good jobs slip through the cracks.

Then reframe the problem:

> Most tradies are told they need more leads. But if calls are missed, replies are slow, and quotes are not followed up, more leads just create more chaos.

Then present the offer:

> Tradie Front Desk installs and manages the front-desk system that captures enquiries, replies fast, follows up, and helps turn quote requests into booked jobs.

## Sales Call Structure

### 1. Diagnose The Current Process

Ask:

- Where do new enquiries come from right now?
- What happens when you miss a call?
- What happens when someone fills out a website form?
- Do Facebook, Instagram, or Google messages get checked daily?
- How quickly do new enquiries usually get a reply?
- Who follows up quotes?
- Where are old leads or old quotes stored?
- Do you have a clear list of active opportunities?

### 2. Identify Leaks

Look for:

- missed calls with no reply
- delayed website form follow-up
- no no-response follow-up
- no quote follow-up
- social messages ignored
- old database unused
- reviews not requested
- campaign leads handled manually
- no pipeline visibility

### 3. Quantify The Cost

Ask:

- What is an average job worth?
- What is a good quote worth?
- How many calls do you miss in a week?
- How many quotes go unfollowed each month?
- What would one extra booked job per month be worth?

### 4. Present The System

Use:

> Based on what you have told me, the issue is not just lead volume. It is that enquiries are coming in without a reliable front-desk process behind them. The system we would build catches missed calls, replies fast, follows up, and keeps opportunities organised.

### 5. Recommend Package

Use audit findings:

- missed calls only: Front Desk Starter
- quote follow-up and pipeline: Booked Jobs System
- low enquiry volume plus broken handling: Managed Growth System after core setup
- old database: add reactivation
- weak reviews: add review builder
- weak website: add website enquiry upgrade

## Objection Handling

### "Is this just lead generation?"

No. Lead generation is only one possible growth layer.

Response:

> The core system is about what happens after someone contacts you. Calls, forms, texts, social messages, old leads, and campaign leads all need fast reply and follow-up. We fix that process first.

### "I already get enough leads."

Response:

> That is exactly why this can be valuable. If you already get enquiries, the first opportunity is making sure fewer of them are missed, delayed, or forgotten.

### "I have tried agencies before."

Response:

> That is why we do not lead with ads. Agencies often focus on sending more enquiries. We focus on whether your business answers, follows up, and books the enquiries properly.

### "I do not want AI talking to customers."

Response:

> AI is optional. We can start with approved SMS, email, forms, missed-call text-back, and simple follow-up. If AI phone or messaging makes sense later, it is controlled and tested.

### "Will this create more work?"

Response:

> It should reduce chasing. The point is not to give you another dashboard to babysit. The point is to make sure enquiries get a fast response, follow-up happens, and you are notified when you need to act.

### "Can you guarantee more jobs?"

Response:

> We cannot honestly guarantee exact job numbers because market demand, pricing, reputation, availability, and closing all matter. What we guarantee is the system: the agreed setup gets built, tested, launched, and fixed if a proposal-listed part is not working.

### "Why is there a setup fee?"

Response:

> Setup is where the system is built around your business: services, messages, forms, missed-call handling, booking flow, follow-up, pipeline, testing, and launch. It is not a software subscription; it is a business system being installed.

### "Why is there a monthly fee?"

Response:

> The monthly fee covers management, support, monitoring, updates, reporting, and optimisation. A front-desk system needs to stay maintained as your services, offers, and enquiry flow change.

### "Can I just pay per lead?"

Response:

> That is a different model. Pay-per-lead usually means someone else controls the source. Our model helps you own the enquiry process and convert the opportunities that come into your business.

### "What if I already have a receptionist?"

Response:

> That can work well. The system can support your receptionist by catching missed calls, organising enquiries, sending follow-ups, and giving them a clearer process instead of replacing them.

### "What if I already use Tradify, Fergus, ServiceM8, Simpro, or similar?"

Response:

> Those tools usually help manage jobs once they are real jobs. Tradie Front Desk focuses on the enquiry stage: capture, reply, qualify, book, and follow up. Where practical, we can design the process to sit alongside your current tools.

## Words To Avoid In Sales Calls

Avoid leading with:

- "We build GoHighLevel accounts."
- "We automate your workflows."
- "We install AI agents."
- "We do omnichannel messaging."
- "We build funnels."
- "We guarantee leads."

Use instead:

- "We catch missed enquiries."
- "We reply fast."
- "We follow up quotes."
- "We help book callbacks and site visits."
- "We organise opportunities."
- "We help you own your pipeline."

Tradie Front Desk should sound like a practical business system, not a marketing promise.
