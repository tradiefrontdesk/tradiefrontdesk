# 1. Purpose Of This Document

This document is the practical checklist system for implementing **The Tradie Front Desk System**.

It turns the business strategy, sales process, technical build, onboarding process, and delivery SOP into repeatable checklists.

Use this document when:

- Preparing for sales calls
- Running Free Front Desk Audits
- Sending proposals
- Closing clients
- Collecting onboarding details
- Collecting access
- Building GoHighLevel systems
- Testing workflows
- Launching clients
- Managing monthly service
- Handling support
- Managing change requests
- Identifying upsells
- Collecting case studies
- Offboarding clients

The goal is to make delivery consistent, clear, and scalable.

Internal rule:

# **If it is important, it should be on a checklist.**

# 5. Audit Score Checklist

Use this to score the business out of 100.

## Category 1: Missed Call Handling — 20 Points

- [ ] Calls are answered consistently — 5 points
- [ ] Missed calls receive instant text-back — 5 points
- [ ] Missed calls are logged — 5 points
- [ ] Urgent missed calls are escalated — 5 points

## Category 2: Website Enquiry Capture — 15 Points

- [ ] Clear quote/contact CTA — 3 points
- [ ] Mobile-friendly form — 3 points
- [ ] Form asks useful questions — 3 points
- [ ] Customer receives instant acknowledgement — 3 points
- [ ] Owner/team receives notification — 3 points

## Category 3: Speed Of Response — 15 Points

- [ ] New enquiries acknowledged quickly — 5 points
- [ ] Customer replies monitored — 5 points
- [ ] After-hours process exists — 5 points

## Category 4: Lead Qualification — 15 Points

- [ ] Service needed captured — 3 points
- [ ] Location captured — 3 points
- [ ] Urgency captured — 3 points
- [ ] Photos/details requested — 3 points
- [ ] Bad-fit leads filtered or reviewed — 3 points

## Category 5: Follow-Up — 15 Points

- [ ] New leads followed up — 5 points
- [ ] No-response leads followed up — 5 points
- [ ] Follow-up is systematic, not memory-based — 5 points

## Category 6: Quote Follow-Up — 10 Points

- [ ] Quotes are tracked — 3 points
- [ ] Quotes are followed up — 4 points
- [ ] Quote outcome is recorded — 3 points

## Category 7: Pipeline Visibility — 10 Points

- [ ] Enquiries tracked in one place — 3 points
- [ ] Opportunity stages are visible — 3 points
- [ ] Owner knows next action — 2 points
- [ ] Won/lost outcomes tracked — 2 points

## Score Interpretation

- 0–30: Major enquiry leaks
- 31–50: Basic process exists but inconsistent
- 51–70: Working process with clear gaps
- 71–85: Good system with optimisation opportunities
- 86–100: Strong front-desk process
