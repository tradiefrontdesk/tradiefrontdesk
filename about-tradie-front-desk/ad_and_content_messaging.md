# 2. Core Messaging Strategy

## 2.1 The Big Idea

# **Tradies are not just losing jobs because they need more leads. They are losing jobs because enquiries are not being handled properly.**

This is the core education message.

Most tradies think they need more leads.

The message should challenge that gently:

> Before you spend more money getting enquiries, make sure your business can capture, respond to, and follow up with the enquiries it already receives.

---

## 2.2 The Main Enemy

The enemy is not competitors directly.

The enemy is:

- Missed calls
- Slow replies
- Forgotten follow-ups
- Quote requests going cold
- Shared leads
- Manual admin
- Messy enquiry handling
- No pipeline
- No front-desk system

Messaging should frame these as the real problems.

---

## 2.3 The Main Mechanism

The mechanism is:

# **Capture → Respond → Qualify → Book → Follow Up**

This should appear across ads, videos, content, and landing pages.

---

## 2.4 The Main CTA

The main CTA should be:

# **Book a Free Front Desk Audit**

Other CTA variations:

- Find where your enquiries are leaking
- Check if missed calls are costing you jobs
- Get your Front Desk Score
- See what your enquiry process is missing
- Book a free enquiry leak review
- Find out what to fix first

---

# 3. Messaging Rules

## 3.1 Do Not Lead With Ads

Avoid front-end messaging like:

> We run Facebook and Google ads for tradies.

Instead say:

> We help tradies capture, respond to, follow up with, and book more of the enquiries they already get — and if they need more volume, we can feed the system with campaigns.

---

## 3.2 Do Not Lead With AI

Avoid:

> AI agents for tradies.

Instead say:

> A smart front-desk system that replies fast, follows up, and keeps job enquiries moving.

---

## 3.3 Do Not Lead With CRM

Avoid:

> We set up your GoHighLevel CRM.

Instead say:

> We give your business one simple place to see new enquiries, callbacks, quotes, and follow-ups.

---

## 3.4 Do Not Promise Guaranteed Jobs

Avoid:

> Guaranteed 30 jobs per month.

Instead say:

> We help stop good opportunities slipping through missed calls, slow replies, and poor follow-up.

---

## 3.5 Use Plain Tradie Language

Use:

- Missed calls
- Quote requests
- Booked jobs
- Enquiries
- Follow-up
- On the tools
- Customer replies
- Job pipeline
- Callback
- Site visit
- Admin
- Chasing

Avoid:

- Conversion infrastructure
- Omnichannel automation
- AI-powered workflows
- SaaS dashboard
- Funnel optimisation
- Lead magnet architecture

---
