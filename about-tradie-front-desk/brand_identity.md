# 1. Purpose Of This Document

This document defines the brand identity for **The Tradie Front Desk System**.

It is designed to become the brand rulebook for how this business should look, sound, sell, and position itself in the market.

This document covers:

- Brand strategy
- Brand name direction
- Positioning
- Category design
- Tagline options
- Core messaging
- Tone of voice
- Brand personality
- Visual identity
- Colour palette
- Typography direction
- Logo direction
- Icon direction
- Website style
- Ad style
- Social media style
- Words to use
- Words to avoid
- Brand one-liners
- Elevator pitch
- Mission
- Values
- Differentiation
- Proof direction
- Brand rules

The goal is to create a brand that does not feel like a generic AI agency, ad agency, or lead seller.

It should feel like a practical, trustworthy, trade-specific system that solves a real business problem.

The brand should make the right prospect think:

> These people understand how trade businesses actually lose jobs, and they have a practical system to fix it.

# 2. Brand Foundation

## 2.1 Current Working Brand Name

# **The Tradie Front Desk System**

This is the current strongest working product name.

It clearly communicates:

- It is for tradies
- It is a front-desk function
- It is a system, not random software
- It handles enquiries and customer flow
- It is practical, not hype-based

---

## 2.2 Recommended Brand Architecture

There are two possible brand structures.

---

## Option 1: Product-Led Brand

Main brand:

# **Tradie Front Desk**

Product:

# **The Tradie Front Desk System**

This is simple, category-driven, and memorable.

Example:

> Tradie Front Desk installs and manages The Tradie Front Desk System for trade businesses.

Best if the business wants to focus only on this niche and product.

---

## Option 2: Parent Brand + Product Brand

Parent brand:

# **System Pros AI**

Product:

# **The Tradie Front Desk System**

Example:

> System Pros AI presents The Tradie Front Desk System — a managed enquiry and follow-up system for trade businesses.

Best if the parent company may later sell other AI systems to other industries.

---

## 2.3 Recommended Structure

Recommended:

# **Use System Pros AI as the parent company and The Tradie Front Desk System as the product brand.**

Reason:

- System Pros AI can stay broad
- The Tradie Front Desk System can be niche-specific
- The offer feels clear to tradies
- You can later launch other industry systems
- You avoid locking the whole company into trades only

Recommended phrasing:

> The Tradie Front Desk System by System Pros AI

or

> Built by System Pros AI

# 4. Brand Category

## 4.1 Category Name

# **Front Desk System For Tradies**

This category is better than:

- AI automation
- CRM setup
- Lead generation
- Ads management
- Chatbot service
- Funnel system

Because it speaks to what tradies understand:

> Who handles the customer when I cannot?

---

## 4.2 Category Creation Statement

> Most tradies do not need another app or another lead seller. They need a front-desk system that captures enquiries, replies fast, follows up, and keeps job opportunities moving.

---

## 4.3 Category Comparison

The category should be positioned against:

| Old Category | Problem | New Category |
|---|---|---|
| Lead sellers | You still chase manually | Front-desk system captures and follows up |
| Ad agencies | More leads, but no handling system | System first, campaigns second |
| CRM software | Another login to manage | Managed system built for you |
| AI agencies | Tech-first and confusing | Outcome-first and practical |
| Receptionist/admin | Helpful but still needs systems | Front-desk process before or alongside admin |

# 9. Tone Of Voice

## 9.1 Voice Description

The voice should sound like:

> A practical business operator who understands tradies, explains things clearly, and knows how to build systems that make money without overcomplicating the business.

---

## 9.2 Tone Rules

Use:

- Short sentences
- Plain English
- Direct explanations
- Practical examples
- Confident recommendations
- Clear next steps

Avoid:

- Long corporate paragraphs
- Technical jargon
- AI hype
- Overly polished consultant language
- Exaggerated income claims
- Empty motivation

---

## 9.3 Example Tone

Good:

> If you miss a call while you are on the tools, the problem is not that you missed it. The problem is whether the customer gets a response before they call someone else.

Bad:

> Our AI-powered omnichannel lead engagement infrastructure maximises conversion velocity across all inbound acquisition sources.

---

## 9.4 Brand Voice In Sales

Sales voice should be:

- Diagnostic
- Calm
- Consultative
- Challenging when needed
- Honest about fit

Example:

> I would not start with ads yet. Your first issue is that enquiries are not being followed up properly. Fix the front desk first, then feed it with more enquiries.

---

## 9.5 Brand Voice In Ads

Ad voice should be:

- Hooky
- Simple
- Problem-aware
- Direct
- Not hype-heavy

Example:

> Stop sending quotes and hoping.

---

## 9.6 Brand Voice In Customer Messages

Customer-facing messages should be:

- Friendly
- Short
- Helpful
- Not robotic
- Easy to reply to

Example:

> Hi Sarah, thanks for reaching out. What suburb is the job in, and what do you need help with?

# 24. Brand Rules

## 24.1 Rule 1: Lead With The Problem, Not The Technology

Bad:

> We install AI agents and automations.

Good:

> We help tradies stop losing enquiries from missed calls, slow replies, and poor follow-up.

---

## 24.2 Rule 2: Sell The System, Not The Tools

Bad:

> We use GoHighLevel workflows and AI.

Good:

> We build and manage the front-desk process that captures enquiries and follows up with customers.

---

## 24.3 Rule 3: Do Not Sound Like A Lead Seller

Bad:

> We guarantee 50 leads.

Good:

> We help you capture, follow up, and organise your own job opportunities.

---

## 24.4 Rule 4: Do Not Overpromise

Bad:

> Never miss another job again.

Better:

> Reduce missed opportunities from calls, slow replies, and forgotten follow-ups.

---

## 24.5 Rule 5: Keep Tradies In Control

Always reinforce:

- You stay in control
- We build and manage the system
- AI is optional
- Ads are optional
- The system follows your business rules

---

## 24.6 Rule 6: Make It Feel Simple

The buyer should never feel overwhelmed.

Use simple phrases:

- We set it up
- We manage it
- You get notified
- Customers get a reply
- Follow-up happens automatically

---

- Lead with AI hype
