# 1. Business Overview

## 1.1 What The Business Does

The business installs and manages a smart front-desk system for trade businesses.

The system helps tradies capture enquiries, respond quickly, follow up automatically, organise leads, and turn more quote requests into booked jobs.

The business is not positioned as a traditional lead generation agency, advertising agency, AI agency, or software reseller. It is positioned as a **managed front-office growth system** for trade businesses.

In plain English:

> We help tradies stop losing jobs from missed calls, slow replies, poor follow-up, and messy enquiry handling.

The system helps trade businesses manage the full journey from first enquiry to booked quote or job.

This includes:

- Website enquiry capture
- Quote request forms
- Missed-call text-back
- Instant SMS and email response
- Optional WhatsApp follow-up
- Optional AI phone assistant
- Lead qualification
- Booking or callback requests
- Simple job pipeline tracking
- Follow-up sequences
- Quote follow-up
- Old lead/customer reactivation
- Google review request automation
- Optional local paid campaigns
- Monthly management and optimisation

The business gives trade business owners the front-office infrastructure they often do not have time, staff, or technical ability to build themselves.

---

## 1.2 Simple Business Description

**The Tradie Front Desk System** is a done-for-you enquiry, follow-up, and booking system for trade businesses that want more booked jobs without chasing every lead, running ads themselves, or managing complicated software.

If the business needs more enquiries, local campaigns can be added as an optional growth layer, but the core offer is not “we run ads.” The core offer is:

# 3. Market Problem

## 3.1 The Problem With Lead Sellers

Many trade businesses are approached by companies selling leads.

These lead sellers often promise:

- A certain number of leads
- Exclusive enquiries
- Pay-per-lead pricing
- Fast growth
- Guaranteed jobs
- No upfront cost

But the trade business often experiences problems:

- Leads are poor quality
- Leads are shared with multiple businesses
- Leads are not ready to buy
- Leads are price shoppers
- Leads are outside the service area
- The business does not own the customer relationship
- The business becomes dependent on the lead provider
- The lead seller controls the source
- There is no long-term asset created
- The business still has to chase, qualify, quote, and follow up manually

The deeper problem is this:

> A lead is not valuable unless the business can capture it, respond quickly, qualify it, and convert it into a booked job.

Lead sellers sell the input. The Tradie Front Desk System improves the process that turns enquiries into booked work.

---

## 3.2 The Problem With Advertising Agencies

Traditional advertising agencies often focus on campaign performance, clicks, impressions, cost per lead, and reports.

This can be useful, but it creates several issues for tradies:

- The owner does not understand the reports
- The agency focuses on leads, not booked jobs
- The business still misses calls
- The business still has slow follow-up
- The business still has no clean pipeline
- The business still has no consistent quote process
- The owner feels dependent on the agency
- If the ads stop, the opportunities stop
- The system behind the ads may still be broken

The key insight:

> Before a trade business spends more money getting enquiries, it needs a system to handle the enquiries it already receives.

Advertising can help, but only if the follow-up system is strong.

---

## 3.3 The Problem With “AI Agency” Messaging

AI is powerful, but many trade business owners are not actively looking for AI.

They are not saying:

- “I need an AI agent.”
- “I need automation workflows.”
- “I need a CRM with conversation AI.”
- “I need a voice bot.”

They are saying:

- “I missed another call.”
- “I forgot to follow up that quote.”
- “I am too busy to reply.”
- “I need more good jobs.”
- “I hate admin.”
- “I do not know where my enquiries are coming from.”
- “I do not want to chase tyre-kickers.”

If the business leads with AI, it may create resistance.

Common fears:

- The AI will sound robotic
- Customers will hate it
- It will say the wrong thing
- It will be complicated
- The owner will lose control
- It is just another tech gimmick
- It will require setup and learning

Therefore, AI should be framed as part of the system, not the main product.

Better language:

- Instant response system
- Smart front desk
- Missed call saver
- Quote follow-up system
- Booking assistant
- Customer follow-up assistant
- Front desk automation

The customer should buy the outcome, not the technology.

# 7. Core Product Concept

## 7.1 Product Name

# **The Tradie Front Desk System**

## 7.2 Product Promise

> More booked jobs. Less chasing. No tech headaches.

## 7.3 Product Description

The Tradie Front Desk System is a managed enquiry and follow-up system that helps trade businesses capture more opportunities, respond faster, qualify customers, and book more jobs.

It acts like a smart front desk for the business by handling the first layer of customer response and follow-up.

It does not replace the owner. It supports the owner.

It does not turn the business into a software company. It gives the business a simple system for handling enquiries better.

It does not force the owner to run ads. If more enquiries are needed, campaigns can be added and managed as part of the service.

---

## 7.4 Core Mechanism

The core mechanism is:

# **Capture → Respond → Qualify → Book → Follow Up**

### Capture

The system captures enquiries from calls, missed calls, website forms, quote requests, ads, old leads, and customer messages.

### Respond

The system sends an instant response so the customer knows the business has received the enquiry.

### Qualify

The system collects basic job details so the business knows whether the enquiry is worth pursuing.

### Book

The system helps move the customer toward a quote, callback, site visit, or booking.

### Follow Up

The system follows up automatically so good opportunities do not disappear.

---

## 7.5 The Simple Customer Journey

A potential customer needs a tradie.

They search online, visit the business website, click an ad, call the number, or submit a quote request.

Instead of waiting hours for the owner to reply, the system responds instantly.

The customer receives a message, call, or follow-up asking for the right job details.

The enquiry is saved into the pipeline.

If the customer does not reply, the system follows up.

If the customer is ready, they can request a callback, book a quote, or be handed to the business owner.

The owner sees the opportunity clearly without needing to manually track everything across texts, missed calls, emails, and memory.

# 17. Delivery Philosophy

The delivery must be simple and controlled.

The business should avoid giving clients too much complexity.

The owner should not need to understand every automation.

They should understand:

- Where new enquiries go
- How they are notified
- How to accept or reject jobs
- How to view the pipeline
- How follow-up works
- How to request changes

The system should be managed enough that the client does not feel abandoned.

# 18. Boundaries And Expectations

## 18.1 What The System Can Do

The system can:

- Capture enquiries
- Respond instantly
- Send follow-ups
- Ask qualifying questions
- Book quote requests
- Track pipeline stages
- Notify the owner
- Reactivate old leads
- Request reviews
- Help run local campaigns
- Improve enquiry handling

---

## 18.2 What The System Cannot Guarantee

The system cannot guarantee:

- A specific number of jobs every month
- That every enquiry will convert
- That bad offers will work
- That poor service will be fixed
- That customers will always respond
- That low ad budgets will generate high volume
- That the business can handle unlimited work
- That all leads will be perfect

This is important for ethical sales and delivery.

---

## 18.3 Recommended Guarantee

Avoid guaranteeing job numbers unless the business controls enough variables.

Recommended guarantee:

> We guarantee your front-desk system will be installed, tested, and launched so your enquiries are captured, responded to, followed up, and organised — or we continue working until it is live and working as agreed.

Optional stronger guarantee:

> If we cannot get your core enquiry capture and follow-up system live within the agreed setup period after receiving all required access and information, we continue setup work at no extra charge until the core system is live.

# 19. Internal Positioning Rules

These rules should guide all future documents, website copy, ads, and sales scripts.

## Rule 1: Do Not Lead With Ads

Ads are optional. The front-desk system is the core product.

## Rule 2: Do Not Lead With AI

AI is part of the delivery, not the main promise.

## Rule 3: Do Not Sell Leads

Sell enquiry capture, response, follow-up, and booked jobs.

## Rule 4: Keep The Message Simple

Use plain language. Avoid technical terms until needed.

## Rule 5: Focus On The Owner’s Daily Reality

Missed calls, slow replies, quote follow-up, and admin chaos are the pain points.

## Rule 6: Sell Control

The client owns the pipeline and customer database.

## Rule 7: Reduce Effort

The offer must feel easier, not like another job.

## Rule 8: Show The System Visually

The sales material should show the simple flow:

**Enquiry → Instant Response → Qualification → Booking → Follow-Up → Pipeline**

## Rule 9: Use Ads As A Growth Layer

Campaigns are used when the business wants more enquiry volume, but the offer is not dependent on ads.

## Rule 10: Sell The Front Desk Before The Campaign

The first sale is the system. The second sale can be growth.
