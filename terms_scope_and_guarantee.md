# 2. Plain-English Service Description

## 2.1 What The Service Is

The Tradie Front Desk System is a managed enquiry, follow-up, and booking-support system for trade and service businesses.

It helps businesses:

- Capture enquiries
- Respond faster
- Recover missed calls
- Collect job details
- Follow up with customers
- Track opportunities in a pipeline
- Request reviews
- Reactivate old leads, if included
- Route campaign leads, if included
- Support call handling or AI phone, if included

The system may include GoHighLevel, CRM tools, forms, calendars, SMS, email, phone systems, AI assistants, ad managers, website widgets, and other connected tools depending on the package purchased.

---

## 2.2 What The Service Is Not

The service is not:

- A guaranteed job-generation service
- A guaranteed revenue service
- A pay-per-lead service
- A full receptionist service unless separately agreed
- A full advertising agency service unless campaigns are included
- A full website rebuild unless included
- A full SEO campaign unless included
- A full job management system replacement
- A financial, legal, or tax advisory service
- A guarantee that every enquiry will convert
- A guarantee that customers will reply
- A guarantee that ads will produce exact results

---

# 3. Core Service Promise

## 3.1 The Promise

The core promise is:

# **More Booked Jobs. Less Chasing. No Tech Headaches.**

This means the service is designed to improve the client’s enquiry handling process by helping capture, respond to, qualify, organise, and follow up with opportunities.

The promise means:

The promise does not mean:
