# Offer Architecture

## Core Offer

**The Tradie Front Desk System**

One-sentence offer:

> We install and manage a front-desk system for tradies that captures enquiries, replies fast, follows up, and helps turn quote requests into booked jobs.

Core mechanism:

**Capture -> Respond -> Qualify -> Book -> Follow Up**

Core promise:

**More booked jobs. Less chasing. No tech headaches.**

## What The Offer Is

Tradie Front Desk is a managed enquiry, follow-up, and booking-support system for trade businesses.

It helps make sure enquiries from calls, missed calls, website forms, social messages, ads, old leads, and past customers enter a clear process instead of getting lost.

The system can:

- catch missed calls
- send instant replies
- collect job details
- ask qualifying questions
- route urgent jobs
- book callbacks or site visits
- follow up quotes
- reactivate old leads
- ask for reviews
- organise opportunities into a simple pipeline
- connect campaign leads to immediate follow-up

## What The Offer Is Not

It is not:

- a pay-per-lead service
- a shared lead seller
- a pure ad agency
- a GoHighLevel setup sold as software
- a generic CRM implementation
- an AI gimmick
- a guaranteed job machine
- a full receptionist replacement unless specifically listed in the proposal
- a full job management system replacement
- a full SEO or website agency unless specifically listed in the proposal

## Offer Hierarchy

The offer has three layers.

### Layer 1: Core Front Desk

This is the essential system.

It includes:

- enquiry capture
- missed-call recovery
- instant reply
- quote/contact form
- basic qualification
- callback or booking path
- follow-up
- pipeline
- owner notifications

### Layer 2: Managed Service

This is the monthly value.

It includes:

- system monitoring
- message updates
- pipeline checks
- workflow fixes
- reporting
- small optimisation work
- support
- campaign or reactivation management only when listed in the proposal

### Layer 3: Optional Add-Ons

These are optional expansion items. They should not be treated as the main offer on the homepage or sales call.

They include:

- old lead reactivation
- past customer campaigns
- review growth
- website enquiry upgrade
- local campaign booster
- AI phone assistant
- AI messaging support
- social DM response
- Google Business Profile messaging

## Flagship Offer

The flagship offer should be:

**Booked Jobs System**

This should be the default recommendation for most serious trade businesses.

The ladder relationship is:

- Front Desk Starter = leak fixing.
- Booked Jobs System = core flagship offer.
- Managed Growth System = expansion after the core system is working.

Booked Jobs System is the main offer. Managed Growth is not the default starting point; it is an expansion offer for businesses with capacity and a working front-desk process.

Why:

- Starter may be too light for established tradies.
- Growth may be too much if their enquiry handling is not fixed.
- Booked Jobs System focuses on the highest-value middle: capture, respond, qualify, book, follow up, and track.

Flagship positioning:

> For trade businesses that already get enquiries but need a better system to answer, follow up, book quotes, and stop enquiries going unanswered or unfollowed-up.

## Product Ladder

### 1. Front Desk Starter

Purpose:

Stop the most obvious leaks.

Best for:

- smaller trade businesses
- owner-operators
- businesses missing calls
- businesses without consistent instant reply
- businesses that need a simple first system

Outcome:

> Catch missed calls, reply faster, and keep new enquiries from getting lost.

Includes:

- basic Front Desk Audit
- missed-call text-back
- instant enquiry response
- basic quote/contact form
- simple follow-up
- simple opportunity pipeline
- owner notifications
- launch testing
- basic monthly system check

Does not usually include:

- ads
- advanced reactivation
- AI phone
- full website rebuild
- complex integrations
- advanced quote follow-up
- multi-location routing

Buyer-facing line:

> The starter system for tradies who want fewer missed enquiries and faster customer replies without building a full growth machine yet.

### 2. Booked Jobs System

Purpose:

Turn more enquiries into booked callbacks, quotes, site visits, or jobs.

Best for:

- established trade businesses
- 3 to 20 staff
- quote-based work
- businesses with scattered enquiries
- businesses losing quote follow-up
- businesses needing pipeline visibility

Outcome:

> Capture enquiries, qualify job details, book the next step, follow up quotes, and keep opportunities organised.

Includes:

- detailed Front Desk Audit
- enquiry capture across key channels
- missed-call recovery
- instant SMS/email response
- quote request form
- job qualification questions
- callback, quote, or site-visit booking flow
- quote follow-up sequence
- no-response follow-up
- simple job opportunity pipeline
- source tracking only where listed as included
- owner notifications
- review request flow only if specifically listed in the proposal
- monthly management
- monthly reporting summary

Buyer-facing line:

> The main front-desk system for tradies who want more quote requests handled properly, less chasing, and a cleaner pipeline of opportunities.

### 3. Managed Growth System

Purpose:

Add more enquiry volume once the front-desk system is ready.

Best for:

- growth-focused trade businesses
- businesses with capacity
- businesses that can handle more work
- businesses ready for campaigns or reactivation
- high-value job niches like roofing, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, solar, landscaping, and renovations

Outcome:

> Build the front-desk system, then feed it with reactivation, reviews, campaigns, and website enquiry improvements.

Includes:

- everything in Booked Jobs System
- local campaign booster only if specifically listed in the proposal
- old lead reactivation
- past customer campaign
- review growth system
- website enquiry upgrade
- AI phone or AI messaging only if selected
- advanced reporting
- monthly optimisation

Buyer-facing line:

> For tradies who want more opportunities coming in and a proper system to make sure those opportunities are followed up fast.

## High-Value Quote Protection

For trades like roofing, solar, renovations, electrical upgrades, drainage, and HVAC, the offer should explicitly protect high-value quote and inspection opportunities.

Buyer-facing line:

> One missed inspection request or unfollowed high-value quote can be worth more than the monthly system fee.

The high-value quote protection flow includes:

- missed-call recovery
- inspection or measure-up request capture
- job type and location qualification
- photo or detail collection
- callback, quote, or site-visit booking
- quote sent follow-up
- no-response follow-up
- owner notification for serious opportunities

Roofing-specific line:

> Capture more roof inspection requests, reply fast, and follow up high-value quotes.

Electrical-specific line:

> Keep urgent fault calls, switchboard quotes, EV charger enquiries, and upgrade requests from going cold.

Plumbing-specific line:

> Route urgent leaks and drainage calls properly while booking non-urgent quotes and site visits.

## Delivery Models

Keep delivery models simple. Do not put all of these on the homepage unless needed.

### Done-For-You

Recommended primary model.

You build, connect, test, launch, and manage the system.

Best for:

- most tradies
- non-technical owners
- busy teams
- businesses that want the outcome without learning software

Public message:

> We set it up and manage it for you.

### Done-With-You

Secondary model.

You build the structure and train the owner, office manager, or admin person to use and maintain parts of it.

Best for:

- businesses with admin staff
- businesses that want more internal control
- lower monthly management situations

Public message:

> We build the system with your team and show them how to run the key parts.

### Build And Hand-Off

Use sparingly.

One-time setup with limited support.

Best for:

- simple jobs
- budget-limited clients
- businesses with technical/admin capability

Risk:

- lower recurring revenue
- higher chance the system is ignored
- lower long-term outcomes

Public message:

> For businesses that want a tightly defined setup and have someone internal to manage it.

## Offer Page Structure

Use this structure for sales pages and proposals:

1. Problem:
   - missed calls, slow replies, poor follow-up

2. Reframe:
   - not more leads first, better enquiry handling first

3. Mechanism:
   - Capture -> Respond -> Qualify -> Book -> Follow Up

4. Offer:
   - managed front-desk system

5. What it includes:
   - buyer-facing outcomes, not technical features

6. Proof:
   - screenshots, metrics, case study, before/after

7. Packages:
   - Starter, Booked Jobs, Managed Growth

8. Guarantee:
   - built, tested, launched, fixed if proposal-listed items do not work

9. CTA:
   - Book a Free Front Desk Audit

## Final Offer Summary

Tradie Front Desk should sell one clear thing:

> A managed front-desk system that helps tradies stop losing jobs from missed calls, slow replies, and forgotten follow-up.

Everything else is a module inside that system.
